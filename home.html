  2. SRI LANKA
    Sri Lanka, a tropical jewel in the Indian Ocean, is a captivating tourism spot renowned for its pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. This island nation offers a diverse range of attractions, from ancient archaeological sites like the UNESCO-listed Sigiriya Rock Fortress to the tranquil tea plantations of the central highlands.

  3. NEPAL
    Nepal, nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, is a mesmerizing tourism spot celebrated for its awe-inspiring landscapes, ancient heritage, and warm hospitality. Home to the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest, Nepal attracts adventure seekers for trekking and mountaineering experiences, with popular trails like the Annapurna Circuit and Everest Base Camp.

  4. PAKISTAN
    Pakistan, a country of diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage, is emerging as an intriguing tourism spot. From the snow-capped peaks of the Karakoram Range to the pristine beaches along the Arabian Sea, Pakistan offers a wide range of natural wonders. The majestic Himalayan and Karakoram mountain ranges attract trekkers and mountaineers, with iconic peaks like K2 drawing adventurers from around the world.
